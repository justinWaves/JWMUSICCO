Bio



Justin Weisberg grew up in the SF Bay area absorbing and studying music at a very young age. At 16 he began to produce computer music. Two years later he signed his first record to Moodswinga recordings while studying Recording Arts and Music Composition under the tutelage of industry veteran Jeff Forehan at West Valley College in Saratoga. Soon after his first record signing he found himself, working alongside multiple Grammy-winning engineer Jason Villaroman, recording and producing his band at the time “Pokerface Protest” at Different Fur Studios in San Francisco. In 2009 Justin left his band to pursue a career as a solo artist and began producing electronic music under the moniker Justin Waves. Under this new alias, Justin Won 1st place in two different remix competitions gaining him a mainstage time slot at Northern Night Music festival and a guest appearance on a popular French podcast for Paris label "Sound Pellegrino". Today he still creates under the moniker Justin Waves, but has expanded his focus to doing more collaborations and producing for other artists as well as his own work. Justin is also an accomplished Guitarist, and pianist that love to play jazz on his spare time. Being very easy to work with and adding a dash of humor and charm to the studio, he is an excellent choice for any collaboration or project where music or record engineering is needed.
